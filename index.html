About Me

I am currently a second year Ph.D. student in the Kelley School of Business Information Systems program at Indiana University. I recieved my Bachelor's and masters degrees from the University of Arizona in Management Information Systems.

Research Interests

My areas of research interest include securing open-source software through the use of network science and novel artificial intelligence approaches including unsupervised graph embedding generation and self-supervised learning.

Recent News

Our paper, on "User Profiling and Vulnerability Introduction Prediction in Social Coding Repositories: A Dynamic Graph Embedding Approach" was accepted and presented at The INFORMS Workshop on Data Science (WDS) 2022.
